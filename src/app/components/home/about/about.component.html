ABOUT THIS TOOL

This tool was designed with the express intent of making PN products more accessible to the entire agency. This easy-to-follow format will help you to learn and familiarize yourself with product terminology, how the product works, why it is beneficial to the client, and ultimately be able to sell premium priced products to clients. At the end of the decision tree process, you will receive a framework scope that will allow for a conversation with your client (and hopefully a sale). It will also provide the senior members of the respective product teams with information they need to quickly turn on designing a product that fits your client’s needs while insuring it is properly priced.

Quick overview of contents included in this tool: 1. Short video tutorials about each of the products 2. Guided questions to understand the client’s needs 3. A scope of work and recommended pricing estimate tailored to the client’s needs 4. An automated process to get an internal expert’s approval of the estimate 5. Infographics and downloadable PDFs to help visualize the product
